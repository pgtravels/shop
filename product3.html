Limited Edition: Converse Upcycled Floral Chuck 70

Description

Our latest upcycling exploration with UK-based vintage retailer Beyond Retro utilizes preloved floral garments to create a new expression of the premium Chuck 70.   • Outsole made with rubber scraps from the footwear manufacturing process
